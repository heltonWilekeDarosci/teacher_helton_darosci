Hello there, and be very welcome!

Hello there! Well, my name is Helton Wileke Darosci. I have been working as an English Teacher for almost years now. I got the interest of studying English because of the games I used to play on my Super Nintendo.
I used to get stuck on the instructions of how to keep going in the game. So I decided to start my path onto this new world. So, I have worked for about 12 years in language schools. I've had great teaching experiences at Wizard in Brusque and also at CCAA, Wizard and KNN schools in São João Batista. These years there have brought me the knowledge and helped me create and shape better classes every day.
Today I work from my home (online) or at yours (in person) where I teach and help teens and adults achieve their professional and personal goals with the support of the English Language.
